About Us

As per the policy directive of the Government of tamilnadu,ELCOT is promoting Information Technology Parks in Tier I and Tier II cities. Information Technology Campuses consisting of Infornation Technology buildings as well as infrastructure facilities are established in Chennai and Tier-II cities such as Madurai,Tiruchirapalli,Hosur,Salem,Coimbatore and Tirunelveli.

Architects were engaged for the creation of common Infrastructure works and construction of IT-cum-Administrative Buildings in the above Specific Economic.
